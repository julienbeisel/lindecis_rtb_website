ring the bell

Inf. to be just what is needed; to hit the spot.


This project started from beats videos, where I had the most fun in a period of time when feeling inspired and motivated making music was a rare thing.

I put all my favorites since the pandemic blended together in a 16 minutes mix, oscillating between short and longer pieces. It went from the idea to the final product in the shortest amount of time, and it was exactly what I needed, what I was looking for: something that was particularly effective, memorable, and familiar, something that just ring the bell.
